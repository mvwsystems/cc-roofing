Our Story

A ROOFING COMPANY
GCs ASKED FOR.

C&C Roofing was founded with a single mandate: bring reliability back to commercial roofing. With a combined 50+ years in multi-family construction, our principals were urged by the general contractors they worked alongside to start a roofing company they could count on.

President Coy Cleveland started his construction career as a superintendent for a multi-family general contractor. He then built a masonry installation business in 2004 — now in its 22nd year. That builder's mindset drives everything we do: quality work, attention to detail, and prompt mobilization when the job is ready.

Today, C&C Roofing serves general contractors and property owners across DFW, Austin, Houston, and all of Texas — with project experience extending into South Dakota and Virginia. We specialize in new construction multi-family projects and are bonded up to $3 million per project.
